# AI-Assisted Phishing Awareness & Compliance Portal  
### GRC Workflow Automation Agent (Proof-of-Concept)

## High-Level Overview

This project is a **working prototype of an AI-assisted GRC workflow portal** designed to simulate how modern organizations manage security awareness training, employee compliance tracking, and audit-ready reporting.

While presented as a phishing awareness training system, the underlying objective is to demonstrate the design and implementation of an **AI-enabled GRC assistant** that reduces manual compliance workload and improves visibility into human risk across the organization.

This project is **modeled after real enterprise compliance workflows** and is built as a proof-of-concept for future **enterprise AI security copilots** that support GRC, security leadership, and audit teams.

## What This Portal Is Designed To Do

The portal simulates a centralized environment where organizations can:

- Deliver phishing awareness training  
- Track employee training completion and performance  
- Maintain compliance evidence for audits (SOX, ISO, SOC2 style scenarios)  
- Provide leadership with real-time compliance posture visibility  
- Reduce manual GRC tracking and reporting effort  

At its core, this system demonstrates how an **AI-driven GRC assistant** could sit on top of compliance workflows and help analysts focus on risk decisions instead of administrative tracking.

---

## How This Helps GRC Analysts

In many organizations, GRC analysts spend significant time:

- Tracking training completion manually  
- Building audit evidence packages  
- Answering repetitive compliance status questions  
- Aggregating data across multiple systems  
- Preparing executive reporting decks  

This portal demonstrates how an intelligent workflow system can:

### Reduce Manual Compliance Tracking
Automates training completion tracking and performance monitoring across employees and departments.

### Accelerate Audit Preparation
Maintains structured training evidence that can be used during audit cycles without manual data collection.

### Improve Risk Visibility
Provides centralized dashboards showing compliance posture and training effectiveness across the organization.

### Enable AI-Assisted Decision Support (Future Extension)
Architecture supports AI capabilities such as:
- Compliance trend summaries  
- Risk-based training recommendations  
- Executive-ready compliance insights  
- Analyst workflow prioritization

## Example Enterprise Use Case Scenarios

### Audit Preparation (SOX / ISO / SOC2 Style)

During an audit cycle, GRC teams must prove:

- Employees completed required training  
- Training effectiveness is measured  
- Evidence is timestamped and traceable  
- Compliance status can be reported at any time  

This portal demonstrates how compliance data can be continuously tracked and structured for audit readiness.

---

### Executive Compliance Reporting

Security and compliance leaders often need quick answers to questions such as:

- What percentage of employees are compliant today?  
- Which departments are highest risk?  
- Are training initiatives improving behavior?  

This system demonstrates how a centralized portal can provide leadership-ready compliance visibility without manual reporting preparation.
